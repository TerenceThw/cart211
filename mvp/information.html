Artist Statement

As an artist, I like to explore where tech and human perception meet. My work takes digital data and turns it into immersive visual experiences, giving people a chance to rethink how they connect with the digital world. Through interactive installations and generative art, I blur the line between the virtual and the physical, creating moments where tech becomes a tool for emotional and sensory vibes.

I work with coding, sound design, and experimental setups, pushing the limits of traditional art forms. With a mix of chance and user interaction, every piece takes on its own life, echoing the ever-changing rhythm of our digital existence.
