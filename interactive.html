This artwork transforms recorded sounds into data that controls the movement of over a million tiny particles. Each particle dances to the rhythm of sound, creating a captivating visual experience.

In this piece, the keyboard transforms into a creative instrument, where each key unlocks unique functions. Users can craft patterns, expand them, or pause the flow, creating a tapestry of generative art that reflects the spontaneity of the creative process.

This piece explores deformation through the interaction of a growing flower and a MIDI controller. As the flower twists and transforms, it slowly loses its recognizable form.

This work uses sound as a tool to create interactions. Videos are mixed and matched whenever a kick drum hits. Blobs are used to track object movement.
